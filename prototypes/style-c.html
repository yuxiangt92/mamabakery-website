Our Story

Baked by a mama who gets it

I started baking because I wanted my own kids to have cake they could love and I could feel good about — no refined sugar, no artificial dyes, no compromise on the fun.

Now I make those happy, healthy cakes for families all over West LA. Every order is small-batch and made fresh, just for your celebration.

— with love, Mama 💕
