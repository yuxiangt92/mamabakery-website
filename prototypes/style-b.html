Our Story

From one mama's kitchen to your celebration

It began when I wanted my own children to enjoy birthday cake without the sugar crash or mystery ingredients. So I learned to bake it better — organic, fruit-sweetened, and genuinely good for growing bodies.

Now I bake for families all over West LA. Everything is small-batch, made to order, and free from artificial dyes, preservatives, and refined sugar.

— with love, Mama
