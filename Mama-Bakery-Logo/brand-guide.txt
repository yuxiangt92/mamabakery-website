TYPE
------------------------------------------------------------
Display / wordmark : Baloo 2 (700)        — rounded, friendly
Body / labels      : Nunito (500/600/700) — clean, warm
Both free on Google Fonts.

------------------------------------------------------------
USAGE
------------------------------------------------------------
• Keep clear space around the logo equal to the cherry's height.
• Use the reversed lockup on amber or photos; never the dark
  wordmark on a dark background.
• Don't recolor, stretch, or add effects to the mark.
• Minimum size: badge 32px, icon 24px.
